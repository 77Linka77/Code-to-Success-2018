The World is Beautiful! Just Catch The Moment!

Most of my photos were taken in the summer time, and now when I'm thinking about it I feel that it's because every summer day is full of light and it shows that life is beautiful and it's everywhere. And in the moments of sadness and melancholy, these images make you understand that beside grey days, also exists sunny ones, that are full of colour, smiles and happiness. I think that this is what photography means to me.
